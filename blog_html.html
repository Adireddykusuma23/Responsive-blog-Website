Mussoorie

There are many things which make Mussoorie a famous hill station in Uttarakhand. Cable car rides, Kempty Falls, Landour Bazaar and Mall Road are popular tourist places.

To catch the snowfall though, visit it between mid-December to mid-February. Strolling on the Mall Road & enjoying ice skating at Jaypee Resort’s Rink are top activities during winters.

Auli

Auli is the prime attraction of winter tourism in Uttarakhand. It’s the top skiing destination in India during the winters, when it’s slopes are blanketed with snow.

Go skiing, snowboarding, enjoy the longest cable car ride & views of Nanda Devi.To catch the snowfall though, visit it between January to February
